In order to share this story, Cend had to be redesigned for client confidentiality. Through this process, I gained valuable insights into how brand identity can be visually addressed in the digital product, both with broader strokes such as colour and font, but also less tangible qualities such as nuanced motion and grid systems.

The Project Shaped Series

Narrative

Alongside storyboarding and writing the script, I edited and animated the sequences shown in the film. Taking inspiration by famous Apple product launch films, I was careful to consider that the objective of this video was not just to illustrate the product, as many of the previous films for Bain had been, but to tell the entire process story. By creating wireframes that grew and evolved, I illustrated iteration and change without investing too much into charts and data.

Motion

Motion design was themed to heighten the imporvements introduced by the Bain team, including the fluid and simple sign-up process. Editing shots to sound gave the film a pop feel and kept the audiences engaged and excited. Further beauty shots enhanced the overall immersion of the film and placed the product in the real world, or less 'protoype-y' settings.
