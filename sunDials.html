Sun Dials¬∑ IB Visual Arts Final Exhibition

2015.




Overview

Completed for the IB Visual Arts Program, Sun Dials is a multimedia exploration into the relationships between time and space, as well as the importance of negative space and the anti-object. By exploring the ways in which both space and time can be interpreted, Sun Dials offers a glimpse into how new perspectives allow for unique ways of eroding and expanding traditionally concrete concepts.

Special thanks to Marina Hinves, Andrew Pawley, and Newington College for all of their support.
